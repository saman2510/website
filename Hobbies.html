My Favourite Hobbies

  • Hiking

    • Hiking is typically defined as a long, vigorous walk, especially on trails or in natural environments. It often involves traversing varied terrain, such as mountains, forests, or parks, and is usually associated with outdoor recreation.

  • Cycling

    • Cycling, also known as bicycling or biking, is the activity of riding a bicycle or other types of pedal-driven human-powered vehicles such as balance bikes, unicycles, tricycles, and quadricycles. Cycling is practised around the world for purposes including transport, recreation, exercise, and competitive sport.

  • Gaming

    • the activity of playing computer games (= games in which the player controls moving pictures on a screen by pressing buttons): The company aims to break into the online gaming market. The film company has invested heavily in the growing gaming business.

  • Painting

    • Painting is defined as the process of applying paint, or another medium, to a solid surface - usually a canvas. Paints or other forms of color are commonly applied to using a paintbrush. However, artists do use different tools such as sponges, spray paint, or even knives.

  • Badminton

    • Badminton is a racquet sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net, where two or four players (singles or doubles, respectively) hit a shuttlecock over the net and into the opponent's half of the court.
